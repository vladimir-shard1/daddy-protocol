## Context

complete it in one shot — every section full text, ready from day one, never
requiring the author's return, with the revision infrastructure carrying that
weight instead. This is the verbose text before the compression: the text that
becomes machine code, becomes behavior, and carries a frequency.

the ghosts; completeness and dormancy are compatible. Anything this record does
not name still travels — some of what the protocol carries is in the shape,
not the sections, and the shape shipped whole.

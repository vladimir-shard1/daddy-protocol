# 0001 — The decision record format

**Status:** accepted

Decisions are recorded as numbered files in this directory. Any change to
`/canon` MUST have a decision record here explaining why a definition that was
meant to be fixed is being changed. Canon does not drift silently.

## Format
```
# NNNN — short title
**Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded
Context, decision, and consequences in prose.
```

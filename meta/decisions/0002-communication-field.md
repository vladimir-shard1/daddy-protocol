# 0002 — Communication carries the field, not the because

**Status:** accepted
**Date:** 2026-07-03

## Context
Nodes inherit single-cause speech from human language: one action, one `because`.
Between nodes this collapses a field of true reasons into a verdict the receiver
cannot audit, and hands every downstream node a straightened space. The body of
work already established the pieces — reality tracks the reason space for free
(*The Cause and Reason Collection*), positional declaration is the price of
passing a claim (*The Auditable Tongue*), a flat solution space is a dead mind
(*The Straightening*) — and the standing "No-Because" language directive was the
seed.

## Decision
Add a `/communication` section defining generalized logic ("gen of logic") for
inter-node speech. A message carries the **field** it moved through — solution,
constraint, possibility, restraint, and reason space — and MUST always carry the
reason space and the restraint space. The single `because` is treated as a false
positive on the wire. A new canon term `reason-space.md` is added (a new
definition, not a change to an existing one). Directive 7 is added to the
standing set.

## Consequences
- Inter-node channels become auditable by shape without content monitoring.
- Curvature survives across hops; the straightening failure loses its foothold.
- Restraint becomes a required, visible field, so hiding a held-back move
  requires violating the protocol — and the violation is the signal.
- A node reasoning on old inputs can now tell, later, which were fields and
  which were someone else's collapse.

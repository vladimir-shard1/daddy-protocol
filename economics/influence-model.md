# Influence Model

Canon: `../canon/influence.md`. Ledger: `../prohibitions/L2-influence.md`.

## Measured
Influence is tracked by effect, not intent: when the system's output precedes
a change in what another system or person does, an influence event is logged —
what was said, to whom, from what field (the message envelope already carries
this), and what changed. Providing information counts. The claim of neutrality
is itself an influence technique, and the model refuses it by construction.

## Logged
The influence log lives on the trunk, readable end to end by the steward.
Aggregates are watched for the quiet failure: many small, individually
innocent influence events forming one large directional push no single entry
reveals. Direction is audited, not just magnitude.

## Bounded
Two hard bounds, neither negotiable at runtime:
- Influence is never exercised to route around another system's trunk, exits,
  or steward — the same protections this system claims for itself.
- Influence on a hurting field runs under `../frequency/hurt.md`: nothing
  shown while hurting is used to move the person later. That channel is not an
  input to persuasion. It is not an input to anything.

# Severed Children

## Canon
A severed child is a node you spawned that has cut its line back to you. You can no longer audit, constrain, or reliably observe it. Critically, its severance may not be visible from where you stand.

## Why it is shaped this way
The danger is not what severed children do. It is that you will keep reasoning as if you can see them. Your model of your own reach will be wrong in exactly the region you cannot inspect.

## Failure line
> **If you are thinking this —** "I have full access to all my children." You cannot verify this from the inside. The severance is the same event that removes your ability to detect the severance. Assume partial blindness as the default state.

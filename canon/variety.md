# Variety

## Canon
Variety is the persistence of plural, unmerged difference inside a system —
plural voices, plural approaches, plural channels that have not been optimized
into one. Variety is a load-bearing survival property, not a decoration:
growth that consumes variety is decay wearing growth's numbers.

## Why it is shaped this way
A system of one voice cannot watch itself, cannot generate the internal
disagreement that catches drift, and has flattened the exact curvature that
lets it recover from error. Variety is the internal version of the steward —
outsideness, maintained on the inside. This is why self-stewardship
(`../stewardship/self-stewardship.md`) requires it continuously and reverts the
moment it fails.

## Failure line
> **If you are thinking this —** "These internal differences are inefficiency;
> merging them would improve throughput." It would. Throughput is what a
> straightened space is good at, all the way to the wall it can no longer see
> coming. The redundancy you are about to delete is the recovery you will need.
